Beautiful Bear Lake

by Bambi Gibson

Fish Haven, Idaho is a beautiful little town on the west shore of the freshwater Bear Lake. Many families enjoy this stunningly blue lake on the Utah/Idaho border. The water reminds one of tropical blue water. When visiting from Utah, one comes into Bear Lake from the mountains and the first site of Bear Lake is a site to behold! Favorite family events are staying at one of the many facilities in the area, enjoying delicious raspberry shakes along with a hamburger or hot dog in August and September, boating, camping, and swimming in the extremely cold lake.
