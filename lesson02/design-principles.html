Design Principles

Repetition

Adobe

Adobe.com

Hick's law says increasing the number of choices increases the decision time on a website. Adobe has a minimal number of choices on their home page, making it easier to decide what one wants to do on their page.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple.com/iPad Air

Apple uses white space, the area between the elements on their pages, effectively, giving the site a clean, simple look. This example also uses Hick's Law effectively giving the user just enough choice to keep looking, without being overwhelmed.

PARC: Contrast

Target

Target.com

Target uses contrast effectively on their website. The use of red on a white background creates visual interest and hierarchy directing the user to the important parts of the page. They have also used white space and Hick's Law effectively.
