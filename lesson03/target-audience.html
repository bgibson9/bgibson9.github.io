Target Audience

Persona 1: Farmer
Demographics
  • 45 years old
  • Married
  • 5 Children
  • Works full-time and farms
  • Household income $40,000 per year
  • Commutes to Monsanto
  • Uses a cell phone and computer to access weather daily for commute and farming activities
Persona 2: Visitors to the Area
Demographics
  • 25-75 years old
  • Married
  • Extended Family
  • Workers and Retired
  • Household income $55,000 per year
  • Engage in camping, hiking, fishing, boating type activites
  • Uses a cell phone and computer to access weather daily to plan outdoor activities
Scenario 1: Preston, ID

Residents and visitors to Preston, “Where the city meets the mountains” often visit the Cub River Canyon recreation area and would need the weather for their camping, hiking, fishing, and hunting activities.
Scenario 2: Preston, ID

Farmers and ranchers in the area would need weather data to manage their crops and livestock. Drivers in the area would like to be able to anticipate road conditions based on the weather.
Scenario 3: Soda Springs, ID

Soda Springs residents would need current weather conditions for their daily activities such as driving and participation in outdoor activities.

Scenario 4: Soda Springs, ID

Soda Springs bills itself as “The Outdoors at Your Front Door.” Hunters, fishermen, hikers, campers, and other outdoor enthusiasts need the Southeastern Idaho accurate local weather forecast as they visit the Caribou National Forest and local “soda” springs and geysers.

Scenario 4: Fish Haven, ID

Fish Haven, Idaho sits at the edge of beautiful Bear Lake. Bear Lake is a popular fishing spot. Visitors would need weather data to make informed camping, ATV riding, fishing, and boating activities on Bear Lake.
